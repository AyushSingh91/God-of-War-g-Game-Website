Kratos

Once a brutal Spartan captain, Kratos led conquests across Greece until a desperate battle with Barbarians forced him to strike a deal with Ares, committing horrific acts in his name. Deceived into killing his own family, he was cursed as the "Ghost of Sparta," their ashes forever on his skin. Seeking redemption, Kratos served the Olympian gods for ten years, growing to hate them. After defeating Ares to become the "God of War," his fury against the gods triggered battles that would decide Greece’s fate.
